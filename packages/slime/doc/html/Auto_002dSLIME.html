8.3 Connecting to SLIME automatically

To make SLIME connect to your lisp whenever you open a lisp file just add this to your .emacs: 

     (add-hook 'slime-mode-hook
               (lambda ()
                 (unless (slime-connected-p)
                   (save-excursion (slime)))))
